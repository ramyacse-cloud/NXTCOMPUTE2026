Conference Guidelines

Participation
  • Only registered participants are allowed to attend and present.
  • All participants must join on time as per the shared schedule.
  • Presentation slots are allotted in advance and cannot be changed.
  • Certificates will be issued only to registered participants who attend and present.
  • The conference follows academic and ethical standards. Plagiarism, copied content, and duplicate submissions are not accepted.
  • Registration fees are non-refundable.
  • The review committee’s decision on acceptance or rejection is final.
  • Selected papers may be published in a Google-indexed journal and publication charges, if any, are borne by the authors.
Paper Submission Rules
  • Papers must be original, unpublished, and not under review elsewhere.
  • Plagiarism must be below 15%.
  • Abstract should not exceed 250 words and must include 3–5 keywords.
  • Full paper length: maximum 8 pages.
  • File format: .doc or .docx only.
  • Font: Times New Roman; size 12 pt; title 14 pt bold; headings 12 pt bold.
  • Single spacing, justified alignment, 1 inch margins on all sides.
  • Paper structure: Title, Author Details, Abstract, Keywords, Introduction, Methodology, Analysis, Findings, Conclusion, References (APA format).
  • Maximum two authors per paper. If both attend, both must register individually.
  • File naming format: AuthorName_Title.docx
  • Send both abstract and full paper to the conference mail id.
